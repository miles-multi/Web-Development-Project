What is Omori?

OMORI is an indie game developed by the team OMOCAT. The game follows the character Sunny and his friends before Sunny moves away. Throughout the game you learn more about why they split up and how Sunny copes with the changes in his life.
The game has a variety of characters, quests, and routes that deepen the story and replayability of the game. This website will give you some general information about this game contained in the marked navigation at the top of the page.
